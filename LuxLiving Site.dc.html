Our Story

crafting luxury
since 2024.

Established in 2024, Lux Living is the authorised Indian home for three world-class furniture houses — aprilstory of China, Italian atelier Ares Italia, and fabric specialist Home Feeling — in 100% genuine leather and premium fabric.

In our first years we've delivered 500+ pieces to 500+ happy homes, each backed by white-glove delivery and an imported care kit. Every piece is engineered to be kept.
